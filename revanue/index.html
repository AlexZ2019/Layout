Detailed Solution Overview: Our Revenue Optimization solutions combine the power of business intelligence, analytic technology with expert consulting services will expose trends, uncover hidden problems and create indexed work lists, which result in much higher reimbursement, elimination of select denials and adjustments while increasing productivity.

By analyzing the ANSI 835 file our technology will uncover and highlight trends that are impacting denials disproportionately so that process improvements can be made; additionally, the 835 file is processed daily to ensure the most current view of all denials and associated work efforts can be trended.
